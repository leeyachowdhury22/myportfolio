Opal

Project Overview

Opal is a money saving tool for those who are looking to keep track of thier monthy expenditure.

Purpose & Context

For this project I was given a project brief and had to deliver an app that was simple to understand and appealed to users that aren’t too familiar with the world of finance.

Approach

User Interviews

Using the Research Learning Spiral I conducted interviews to better understand the users pain points. Here are the main findings from my research.

Flow Diagram

After gathering enough data I developed a user flow diagram that enables users to complete three main tasks: log in securely, create new saving goal and view thier trasactions.

Wireframes

I used the crazy 8's methodology to develop some initial exploratory ideas. Once I was happy with the designs, I then developed some low, mid and high fidelity wireframes.

Retrospective

what went well?

Overall the execution of the project went really well. I was able to develop a colour scheme and typography that aligned well with the tone of the app. After finishing the project, I had my project reviewed by my peers and tutor. Everything went well except for minor issues.

What didn't go so well?

Designing the logo took longer than expected as I was having a hard time settling on one idea. Also my lack familiarity with editing tools put a strain on the design process.

Solution

Put aside some time to watch a few tutorials on how to use tools such as Photoshop.
